📚 Courses & Enrollment

Learn how to find, enroll in, and manage your courses.

Browsing Available Courses

Two ways to explore courses:

  1. By Module: Visit Modules → Select module → Browse courses
  2. By Search: Use the search bar on Courses page to find specific topics

Each course shows difficulty level, instructor name, and enrollment count.

Understanding Course Information
Course Code
Unique identifier for the course
Difficulty Level
Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
Duration
Estimated time to complete the course
Prerequisites
Courses or skills required before enrolling
Learning Objectives
Skills and knowledge you'll gain
How to Enroll in a Course
  1. Click on a course from the courses list
  2. Review the course details and learning objectives
  3. Click the Enroll button
  4. You'll see a confirmation message
  5. Wait for instructor approval (usually within 24-48 hours)
  6. Once approved, the course appears in "My Active Courses"
Note: Most courses require instructor approval. Some open enrollment courses may be accessible immediately.
Managing Your Enrollment

Active Courses:

  • Access course materials and submit assignments
  • View your progress and grades
  • Interact with instructors and peers
  • Earn certificates upon completion

Withdraw from a Course:

  1. Go to "My Active Courses"
  2. Click the course
  3. Click Unenroll (if available)
  4. Confirm your withdrawal

Withdrawing may affect your progress and grades. Contact support before withdrawing.

Search & Filter Courses

Use these filters to find the right course:

  • Keywords: Search by course title or topic
  • Module: Filter by academic module
  • Difficulty: Find beginner, intermediate, or advanced content
  • Instructor: Search courses by specific instructors
