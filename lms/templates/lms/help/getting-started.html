🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to Synego Institute! Follow these steps to start your learning journey.

Step 1: Create Your Account
  1. Click Register on the homepage
  2. Fill in your personal details and create a password
  3. Check your email for verification link
  4. Click the link to activate your account
  5. Wait for admin approval before enrolling in courses
Step 2: Log In
  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. Click Sign In
  4. You'll be directed to your Student Dashboard
Step 3: Browse & Enroll in Courses
  1. Click Browse Courses from your dashboard
  2. Explore available courses by department or difficulty
  3. Click a course to see details, prerequisites, and learning outcomes
  4. Click Enroll to request enrollment
  5. Await instructor approval (usually within 24 hours)
Step 4: Start Learning
  1. Once enrolled, your course appears on your dashboard
  2. Click Open to access course materials
  3. Work through chapters, complete quizzes, and submit assignments
  4. Track your progress in the My Progress section
  5. Complete all coursework to earn your certificate
Tips for Success
  • Set aside regular study time each week
  • Read the course syllabus carefully
  • Submit assignments before the deadline
  • Reach out to instructors if you need help
  • Check notifications regularly for updates
