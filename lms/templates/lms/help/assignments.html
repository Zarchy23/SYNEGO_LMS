View My Assignments

To see all your assignments:

  1. Go to My Courses from your dashboard
  2. Select the course containing the assignment
  3. Navigate to the Assignments section
  4. You'll see all active and completed assignments for that course

Assignments are sorted by due date. Overdue assignments appear in red.

Submitting an Assignment

Steps to submit:

  1. Open the assignment from your course
  2. Click Submit Assignment
  3. Upload your file (PDF, Word, or image formats)
  4. Add any optional notes or comments
  5. Click Submit to finalize
Tip: Make sure your file is properly named and clearly labeled with your name and student ID.
Viewing Feedback & Grades

After your instructor grades your assignment:

  1. Return to the assignment on your dashboard
  2. You'll see your grade displayed at the top
  3. Click View Feedback to read instructor comments
  4. Your grade is also recorded in your My Grades section

You'll receive a notification when your assignment has been graded.

Resubmitting Work

If your instructor marks your work as "Returned" for revision:

  1. Navigate to the assignment
  2. Review the feedback provided
  3. Click Resubmit
  4. Upload your revised work
  5. Submit for re-grading
Note: Resubmissions must be made before the assignment deadline passes.
Late Submission Policy
  • On Time: Full credit available
  • 1-2 days late: 10% deduction
  • 3+ days late: 20% deduction
  • More than 1 week late: May not be accepted

Contact your instructor if you have extenuating circumstances.
