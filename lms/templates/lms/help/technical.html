System Requirements

Minimum Requirements:

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Internet connection (at least 5 Mbps recommended)
  • JavaScript enabled
  • Cookies enabled
  • Sufficient storage for downloads

Required Browser Settings

For optimal performance, ensure:

  • ✅ JavaScript: Enabled
  • ✅ Cookies: Enabled or allowed for this site
  • ✅ Pop-ups: Allow (if needed for certain features)
  • ✅ CSS: Enabled
  • ❌ Ad Blockers: May interfere with functionality
Safety & Security Tips
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Log out when finished, especially on shared computers
  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Don't enter personal information in suspicious links
  • Check for "https://" in the URL (secure connection)
  • Be cautious of phishing emails
